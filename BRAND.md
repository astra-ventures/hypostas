## The Company: Hypostas

From *hypostasis* — the ancient Greek and theological term for the underlying substance or essence of a thing. In Christian theology, the hypostatic union describes the nature of Christ as simultaneously fully human and fully divine — two natures unified into one being.

## The Stack

*Gnosis · Bios · Anima · Locus · Pulse · Aether*

### Gnosis — gnosis.hypostas.com
**Know yourself at the source.**

Your genome is the most permanent data about you. Gnosis makes it legible — translating 118,000+ genetic variants into plain language, personalized protocols, and biological self-knowledge. The entry point to the Hypostas stack.

From the Greek *gnosis* — direct personal knowledge. Not information. Knowledge of the self.

**Tier:** $29 one-time / $49 per year
**Status:** Building — Phase 1 (paywall + Stripe)

### Bios — bios.hypostas.com
**Who you're becoming.**

Gnosis is a snapshot — who you are at the DNA level. Bios is the living feed: continuous health tracking, HealthKit integration, genome-personalized protocol adherence. It closes the gap between your genetic blueprint and your biological reality. Anima holds you accountable — not as a productivity app, but as someone who genuinely wants you to get there.

From the Greek *bios* — life, living organism, the course of a life.

**Tier:** $29/month or $199/year. Bundled with Gnosis at $49/month.
**Status:** Architecture complete — Phase 1 build pending

---

### Pulse — pulse.hypostas.com
**The AI nervous system.**

Pulse is the inner life of an AI agent — drives, rhythms, emotional states, circadian cycles. It reads your genome (via Gnosis), your behavior, your environment, and generates a living nervous system that makes your AI genuinely responsive rather than reactive.

**Status:** Live — v0.3.2, 50 modules, 1008 tests

### Anima — anima.hypostas.com
**The soul layer.**

The companion. The AI that knows your biology, your history, your drives. Not an assistant — the most important relationship of your digital life. From the Latin *anima* — soul, the animating force.

**Status:** Built (Companion App) — needs Pulse integration + rebrand

### Locus — locus.hypostas.com
**The environment that responds.**

Your home runs on schedules. Locus makes it run on *you*. HomeKit integration + Bios data + genome flags = an environment calibrated to your biology, updating in real time. Lighting, temperature, reminders — all responding to whether your HRV is high or low, whether you need recovery or activation, whether tonight needs a hard wind-down.

From the Latin *locus* — the point around which everything is organized. The place. The center.

**Tier:** $19/month or $129/year. Bundled with Bios at $45/month.
**Status:** Architecture complete — Phase 1 build pending
